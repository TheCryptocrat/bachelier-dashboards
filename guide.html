💡 Tips for Best Results

  • Keep it short — USDJPY works better than "show me all USDJPY trades across every strategy"
  • Strategy names — copy exactly as they appear in the data table for best matches
  • For Long vs Short — always include both "long" and "short" in your query
  • Combine filters — live last 30 days or demo March
  • Quick buttons — use the preset buttons at the top of the Explorer page as a starting point
  • Full data table — the table at the bottom of the Explorer can be sorted by any column (click the header) and filtered by text
  • When a query fails — the Explorer will show format suggestions and possible matches you can click
  • Date pickers on other pages — Demo Bots and Live Trading pages have date range pickers (7D, 30D, 90D, YTD, ALL) in the header
  • Line chart dropdowns — use the dropdown above any line chart to isolate a single strategy or account
